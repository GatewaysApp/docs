# Gateways vs Railway

**Railway** focuses on **fast project deploys**, **templates**, and **developer-friendly** service composition on Railway’s platform.

**Gateways** is built for **workspaces, projects, and environments** over **customer-owned** AWS, Azure, and GCP—**diagram-first**, with **resource connections**, **activity**, and **HTTP APIs** for automation.

| Topic | Gateways | Railway |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Billing & boundary** | Your cloud accounts | Railway platform |
| **Team model** | Workspaces, roles, plan gates | Railway projects & teams |
| **Strength** | Honest infra map + multi-cloud ops | Speed from repo to running service |

Pick **Railway** when **time-to-first-deploy** on a **single PaaS** matters most. Pick **Gateways** when **architecture in real clouds** and **cross-team alignment** drive delivery.
